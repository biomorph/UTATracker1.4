ROUTE

This tab finds all vehicles on an inputted UTA route (2,6, 72 etc). For Trax use 701 - Blue Line, 703 - Red Line, 704 - Green Line and 750- FrontRunner.

If the typed route number is valid, a map with pins denoting vehicles' position is displayed.

The pins are color coded red or purple depending on the direction of the vehicle. There are also some trax and frontrunner vehicles without direction information that are displayed as Green pins

The map also displays the route in red. If you click the vehicle pins you can see the name of the route and the direction the vehicle is traveling. Also displayed is the vehicles' "on-time" status (colored dot)

Light Gray Colored Dot : Vehicle is > 10 min early

Blue Colored Dot : Vehicle is early

Green Colored Dot : Vehicle is < 5 min late

Orange Colored Dot : Vehicle is 5-10 min late

Red Colored Dot : Vehicle is > 10 min late

White Colored Dot : No info available

The map also shows a button (magnifying glass) that searches for the timetable.

The map also shows a bookmarks button, to add the displayed route to your favorites.

CLOSEST

This tab finds the 25 closest stops  to your current location and the routes/buses that stop there in the next 30 minutes.

You can also filter the results to show only the route your are interested in (type this in the filter by this route field).

If the typed route is valid, a map displaying closest stops that the route stops at are displayed on the map as pins.

Selecting the refresh button on the map refreshes the map and shows the 25 closest stops to the center of the area of the map your are looking at.

Selecting the GPS button on the map shows you the 25 closest stops to your current GPS location

Selecting a pin brings up the stop address (900E @ South Temple etc) and the direction of the stop.

Selecting the arrow on the pin brings up the buses that stop at the selected stop (in the next 30 minutes), direction of the buses, arrival times and their timetables.

BOOKMARKS

This tab displays the routes and stops your bookmarked in the route-show vehicles and the closest stops maps.

Selecting a route entry brings up the route tab, searches for these vehicles and displays them on the map. If a stop entry is selected, buses that are scheduled to arrive at the stop in the next hour are shown.
